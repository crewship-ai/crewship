Design notes

  • Total toolbar height: 56px (row 1: 32px + row 2: 24px). Previously ~110px.
  • Mission name appears once. Status badge lives in row 2 info strip, not repeated.
  • Row 2 uses font-family: monospace for data tokens (tokens, cost, time).
  • Active tab: 2px bottom border flush with row 1 bottom edge — no pill/rounded style.
  • Dropdown: sharp corners, dark overlay, grouped by status with section labels.
  • Row 2 swaps content based on selection: aggregate stats vs mission-specific data.
  • Action buttons shown contextually — hidden in "all missions" state.
  • Status dot in selector animates for IN_PROGRESS missions only.
  • Click outside dropdown or press Esc to close.
